Sports Cell

We are very proud to announce the first ever Esports cell of our college under CoDE. Esport is about passion, energy and excitement and our team is here to fulfill all the terms of eSports. We are the squad of energetic people who are ready with lots of Esports activities to challenge you. The idea behind the Esports cell is to provide gamers a platform to show their gaming skills and to interact with other best gamers from college. Our efforts are to provide the best possible opportunities to aspiring gamers and encourage them to take part in various Esports activities. Everyone need some change and break from daily academic routine and want to try something different right? So what are you waiting for be the part of our overwhelming, exciting and challenging Esports activities and compete with other best gamers.
